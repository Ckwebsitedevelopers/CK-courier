About CK Deliveries

CK Deliveries is a locally owned and operated delivery service founded by Chola Kachamba. We specialize in fast, reliable, and eco-friendly deliveries using bicycles to navigate urban areas efficiently.

Our mission is to provide exceptional delivery services while reducing environmental impact. We understand the importance of timely deliveries and treat every package as if it were our own.

With a team of dedicated cyclists, we ensure your items are delivered with care and speed. Whether it's food, groceries, parcels or important documents, you can count on CK Deliveries.
